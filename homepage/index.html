Calling Me By Name Sign In

Invitation Only

Use of Calling Me By Name is by invitation only.

You will need to demonstrate that you are currently a member of the BYU community.

Suggestion

We suggest that you visit some other BYU application, then return to this page and reload it to try again.

For example, visit Mendix.byu.edu and sign in; then return here and reload this page.

Experimental

If you possess a verifiable credential that states that you are a member of the BYU community, and you are holding it in an agent on your mobile phone, you can use it to identify yourself.

You can obtain a verifiable credential at YCred.byu.edu.
